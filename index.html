  • Funnel Development and Optimization (Clickfunnels, ThriveThemes, etc)
  • Direct Response Copywriting
  • Agile/Scrum Project Management & Development
  • Programming (Java, JavaScript, Ruby, HTML5, CSS)

  • SQL Server Database Foundations
  • Information Marketing & Expert/Consultant Marketing
  • Technical Writing & Knowledge Base Management

As a result, I have been able to advise friends and clients in strategies that, when implemented, have yielded positive results. Like one client who went from $0/month to $30K/month after implementing a new offer and funnel. Or another, who gained an extra $18K/quarter thanks to ops changes. Or the countless others who are still using websites and funnels I created for them or using processes I've suggested.
